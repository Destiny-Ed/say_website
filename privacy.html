Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 12, 2025

At Say, we value your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Name, email, and authentication details (e.g., Google/Apple sign-in) when you sign in.
  • Usage Data: Translation history, chat logs, and app interactions, stored locally or in the cloud with Pro.
  • Device Information: Device type and OS version for analytics and support.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve translation services and cloud sync features.
  • To personalize your experience and offer Pro benefits.
  • To ensure security and comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. It may be shared with service providers (e.g., cloud storage) under strict confidentiality. Data may be disclosed if required by law.

4. Data Security

We use encryption and secure protocols to protect your data. Cloud data is safeguarded with industry-standard measures.

5. Your Choices

  • You can delete chat history or disable cloud sync in settings.
  • Opt-out of analytics via device settings.

6. Children’s Privacy

Say is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect their data.

7. Changes to This Policy

Updates will be posted here with the revised date.

Contact us via e-mail for questions or to exercise your rights.
